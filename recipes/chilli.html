Easy Chilli

Chili, often referred to as chili con carne, is a hearty and flavorful dish that has earned its place as a beloved classic in many culinary traditions, particularly in Tex-Mex and American cuisine.

At its core, chili is a stew-like concoction typically made with ground or cubed beef, kidney beans, tomatoes, and a medley of spices.

Ingedients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chili beans, not drained
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder, or to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sauce.
  4. Bring to a boil and stir in chili powder.
  5. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 15 minutes.
  6. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
